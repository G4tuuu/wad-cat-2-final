About me

Hi! I am Laiwa Anyin Tallo.I am a 20 year old artist based in Nairobi,Kenya and have been practicing for about 7 years;self-taught too! Surrealism and internal expression make my work come to life. I explore various themes in my work including;sexuality, intimacy, politics and many others using my OCs, IRIS and Carmen. I use any media in my line of sight but here, I showcase my exploration with graphite,watercolor,markers,pastels,acrylic on canvas and mixed media.

My why

Years ago, around 2020, I decided I WILL be an artist.I WILL be good at my craft. I started drawing again after years of not, formulating strategies on how to practice, how to get those "10,000 hours" and finally started working being both disgusted and proud of my work; because it was my best piece, yet not what I thought was in my grasp. I sort to SEEK my goals with intention and practice and GRASP my desires by the horns.I CHOOSE to know that I can do better and to be proud of my work!
