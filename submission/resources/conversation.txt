Conversation Summary

I created this portfolio for Laiwa Anyin Tallo, an artist and model based in Nairobi.
 Before starting the project, I discussed the website with her and gathered information about the work she wanted to showcase.

The main goal of the website is to present her artwork, artistic identity, and original characters in a way that feels personal and visually engaging.
 The intended audience includes people interested in her art, potential collaborators, and anyone who would like to learn more about her creative journey.

From our discussions and the materials she shared, I learned that surrealism, internal expression, and storytelling are important themes in her work. 
She also wanted her original characters, Iris and Carmen, to have a dedicated section in the portfolio.

Based on this information, I planned a portfolio-style website with the following sections:

* About Me
* Early Work
* My OCs
* My Why

I chose a blue and silver colour palette to create a calm, artistic atmosphere and used a gallery layout to showcase her artwork.
 The website was designed to be responsive and easy to navigate while keeping the focus on her creative work.
